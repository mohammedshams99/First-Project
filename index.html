Introduction

One of OLAP’s primary goals is to reach our customers’ satisfaction with the products and services provided to them, not only in order for them to return to buy from your store again, but also so that they spread their good experience among their family and friends, which leads to building a large customer base for your store and services, and this is the main reason for establishing a protection policy Seller and customer. You have to make sure that the products you are shipping match the specifications of the products on the Jumia website to earn returns. That's why we started implementing smart product inspection to ensure happy customers. In addition, there is a return policy that guarantees your right as a Jumia partner.

Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is very important to OLAP. Therefore, your name and other information relating to you is used in the manner described in the Privacy Policy. We will collect information as necessary or if it is directly necessary to our dealings with you. We will retain your data if required by law or to use it for the purposes for which it was collected. You can browse the site without having to provide any personal data. Your personal identity remains anonymous throughout your visit to the site and is not revealed unless you have a special electronic account on the site that you access using your username and password. If you have any comments or suggestions, we are happy to receive them at support@olap.com

Cookies

You are not required to activate and accept cookies while visiting our website. But if you want to place an order or add a product to your shopping cart, you must activate the feature in your browser settings to accept cookies (if necessary) in order to be able to use the shopping cart functions and continue with the purchase.
